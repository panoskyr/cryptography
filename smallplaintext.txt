The moral component of the Spaceship Earth metaphor is therefore somewhat paradoxical It casts humans as ungrateful for gifts which in reality they never received And it casts all other species in morally positive roles in  lifesupport system with humans as the only negative actors But we are part of the biosphere and the supposedly immoral behaviour is identical to what all other species do when times  are good  except that humans alone try to mitigate the effect of that response on their descendants and on other species
